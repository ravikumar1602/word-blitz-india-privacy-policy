Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? ▼
We use OTP-based login, so there's no password to reset! Simply open the app, enter your phone number, and we'll send you a new OTP to log in.
Can I change my username? ▼
Yes! Go to Profile → Edit Profile to change your username. Note: You can only change it once every 30 days.
How do I delete my account? ▼
You can delete your account by going to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or email us at ravichaudhary.studio@gmail.com with "Delete Account" in the subject.
Why am I not receiving OTP? ▼
Check if you entered the correct phone number, ensure good network connectivity, and verify that SMS messages aren't blocked. If issues persist, contact support.
How do I report inappropriate content? ▼
If you encounter inappropriate content in the game, please report it immediately through the in-app report feature or email us with screenshots at ravichaudhary.studio@gmail.com.
