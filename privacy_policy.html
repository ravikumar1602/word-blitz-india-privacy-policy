Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 6, 2026  |  Applicable in: India and Worldwide

Welcome to Word Blitz India ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game application. By using Word Blitz India, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone Number — When you register via OTP login
  • Google Account Info — Name, email, profile photo (when using Google Sign-In)
  • Display Name — Your chosen username in the game

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Data — Scores, levels, words found, streaks, achievements
  • Device Information — Device type, OS version, app version
  • Usage Data — Game sessions, features used, time played
  • Advertising ID — For personalized ads via Google AdMob
  • Crash Reports — Error logs for debugging (via Firebase)

1.3 Guest Users

If you play as a guest, we only store a temporary device ID locally. No personal information is collected from guest users.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas (cloud servers)
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are never stored — we use Firebase OTP authentication
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Data is stored on servers located in Singapore/Asia region

5. Children's Privacy

Word Blitz India is suitable for all ages including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Our app is a word puzzle game with educational value — it helps players learn Hindi and English vocabulary.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.
